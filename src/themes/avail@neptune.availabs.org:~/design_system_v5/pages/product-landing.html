The expedition kit

Everything a publisher packs

No plugin roulette. The tools every site needs are stamped into the core — and everything you make stays yours to export.

Section composer

Pages are stacks of sections on a 12-column grid. Drag, span, and reorder in place — no shortcodes, no page-builder plugins.

Living data cards

Bind any section to a dataset. Tables, charts, and cards refresh the moment the data does — no copy-paste republishing.

The theme press

One click re-inks every page. Color, type, and layout tokens travel together, so a redesign is never a rebuild.

Drafts & roles

Writers draft, editors approve, admins publish. Every page keeps its full version history, ready to restore.

Datasets & media

Upload spreadsheets, GeoJSON, and imagery once; reuse them across every page — and every site — you run.

Search & structure

Clean URLs, sitemaps, feeds, and fast full-text search ship with every site. Nothing to bolt on afterward.

The composing room

Compose like a printmaker

The editor is the page. Set a section's span, pick its type style, bind its data — and watch the print come up exactly as readers will see it.

  • Live sources preview with real rows, not lorem
