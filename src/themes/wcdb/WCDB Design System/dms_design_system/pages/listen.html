Where to listen

Over the air
Tune to 90.9 FM anywhere in the Capital Region. Best reception on a real radio with a proper antenna — the way the licence intends.
  • 90.9 MHz · Capital Region
  • ~25 mi · 280 W ERP
  • SUNY Albany · uptown campus
Online stream
A 128 kbps MP3 stream, served straight from the studio. Paste the URL into VLC, foobar2000, or any browser that still respects an audio tag.
  • stream.wcdb.fm/live
  • 128 kbps · MP3 · 44.1 kHz
  • HLS fallback available
