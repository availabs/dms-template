Dispatch · Mar 03 · By Jada R.

Forty-eight years on a hairline.

The station's licence renewal landed last week. A quiet milestone — and a reminder that everything WCDB sends out at 90.9 MHz still arrives via the same hair-thin signal it did in 1977.

We pulled the engineering logs going back to the original FCC filing. Forty-eight years of measurements, repairs, and midnight transmitter calls. Most of it still on paper.
