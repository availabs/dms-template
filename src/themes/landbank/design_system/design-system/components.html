Lexical (rich text)

Disposition policy

How lots are priced

Pricing follows the published disposition policy, not negotiation. Most vacant lots list at a few hundred dollars; see the for-sale list for current asking prices.

  • Side lots: priority to adjacent owner-occupants
  • Buildings: priced from condition and appraisal
"Properties go to buyers with a plan — that's the point of a land bank."

Record identifiers

Parcels are keyed by SBL, e.g. 76.49-6-65:

SBL   = section.block-lot
76.49 = tax map section
6-65  = block 6 · lot 65

Container, header, legend, zoom stack, and popover are themed; tiles come from the platform. Pin colors are the status system — never per-page palettes.

Primitives this theme isn't designed for (audio/video players, calendars) still inherit the neutral recipes above — paper surfaces, ink/10 borders, Public Sans — so nothing renders as unstyled platform default. The parcel plate is the one custom column type this theme registers; it is not a new section component.
