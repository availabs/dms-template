Color

Both brand brights come straight from the logo mark — the sky above the skyline and the hill below it. They are graphic colors: fills, charts, the map. Text and interaction always step down to their darkened pairs so contrast holds. The workhorse ratio is roughly 90% neutrals, 8% sky, 2% leaf.

The status system

Every property in the portfolio carries one disposition status. The same color means the same thing on a pill, a map pin, a donut slice, and a bar — everywhere, always. Pills are the canonical rendering: tinted ground, deep text, no border.

Type

Three voices, fourteen tokens. Archivo — opened up to 118% width — is the display voice: sturdy civic lettering that echoes the wide-tracked caps of the ACLB wordmark. Public Sans, the typeface built for the U.S. public sector, carries all running text. IBM Plex Mono is the ledger voice: parcel IDs, eyebrows, data chrome. Every text treatment on every page resolves to one of these tokens plus modifier axes (color · uppercase+tracking · tabular-nums · italic).

Surfaces & the parcel plate

Five named surfaces from _shared.css, plus the system's signature: the parcel plate, a lot-geometry thumbnail drawn to each parcel's true recorded proportions. Vacant lots don't photograph well — their survey does.
