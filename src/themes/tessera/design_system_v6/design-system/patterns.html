Patterns

Where the primitives have to get along: a filtered table with its chips and pagination, a real form with a real error, the sign-in card, the edit-mode chrome an author lives in all day — plus the small recurring brand moves (the beta chip, the margin notes, the rough-edges board) that make a tessera page recognizable at arm's length.

01 · states

Empty, loading, broken — still on brand

Every data section has three off days. Empty states invite the next action; loading is quiet skeleton bars; errors name the problem and offer the retry. No mascots, no "oops".

empty state

no sections yet

this page is a blank sheet. sections snap to the grid as you add them.

the empty canvas shows the sheet (t6-sheet) — the grid itself is the invitation.

loading state

skeleton bars in bg-well, animate-pulse, shaped like the content they replace. no spinners for whole sections.

error state

couldn't load traffic_counts

the data server didn't answer in 10 seconds. your layout is fine — it's the connection.

brick is the icon and nothing else — the message stays ink/graphite and says what broke.

08 · recurring brand patterns

The small moves that make it tessera

Six little conventions that show up on three-plus pages each. They're rationed on purpose — used everywhere, they'd be noise; used exactly here, they're the handwriting.

the beta chip

beta 0.6.0 v6

rounded-full, border-rule, t-metaXS, graphite. sits after the wordmark on every marketing page and the sign-up card. it names the honest version number — never "new!".

the handwritten margin note

this one is rotated −1° and graphite


and this one +1°, cobalt, smaller

  • · at most 3 per page, ±1–2° rotation
  • · graphite or cobalt only, never status colors
  • · margins and asides — never inside controls, never required reading

the marker highlight

publish puts your draft live in one click, no build step.

t6-mark — a 46%-height marker film behind the words. ≤ 2 per band, prose and headlines only. it's annotation, so it never touches buttons, labels, or nav.

known issues — 0.6.0

csv upload chokes past 50mb

dark mode flashed light on first paint

maps are slow past ~10k features

drawer scroll traps focus on ios

undo history resets on publish

a t6-board card. fixed items keep their row, checked in go — the list is a changelog, not a confession.

the joint squares

t6-joint pins a 7px square tile where rules meet — a tessera set at the joint, zed-style. marketing pages only — product chrome stays quiet.

the keyboard hint chip

search⌘K publish⌘⏎ closeesc

kbd: t-metaXS in a border-rule box, rounded (4px — smaller than controls on purpose). appears in menus, dialog footers, and the docs. cobalt only when it's the highlighted row's hint.
