Deco Field Guide · foundations

Theme tokens

The brand's vocabulary before it is composed into anything: nine inks, twelve type tokens, a badge-style icon registry, and the poster-frame surface recipes. Every page in this system resolves to what is declared here. Sourced from the art-deco park posters and badge series in references/tessera/deco.

Color

One ink, two paper stocks, one brass accent, and five poster pigments. Text is always ink on paper/parchment, or paper/parchment on ink — pigments are for art, badges, and accents, never body text.

Type

Three voices: Josefin Sans display caps (geometric, high-waisted — the poster titling voice), Jost prose and meta (a Futura descendant, period-correct body), and Yellowtail as the brush-script accent — at most twice per page. Twelve tokens; modifier axes (color, italic, tabular-nums, responsive downshift) apply at the call site and never mint new tokens.

Section composer

Folded in: stat numerals and plan prices are displayXL + tabular-nums (in gold on ink) — not a separate numeric token. Step roundel numerals are displayLG. SVG text inside poster art is artwork, not page type, and is exempt from the token contract.
