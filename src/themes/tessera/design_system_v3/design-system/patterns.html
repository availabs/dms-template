Recurring brand patterns

On every page that earns them

Conventions that repeat across product pages — easy to mistake for one-off chrome, so they are pinned here. If a pattern appears on three pages, it appears on all pages of that kind.

Breadcrumbs — above the page header, always
Hazards Flood Suffolk county

Separator is the chevron, never a slash. metaSM; ancestors graphite, current slate. Lives in the slim TopNav strip, above the page-header band, beside nothing.
