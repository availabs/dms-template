tessera / hosted vs self-host

Two doors. Same engine.

Tessera is open-source. You can run the engine yourself, on any modern Linux host with a Postgres database. We also operate the hosted service for teams who would rather place tiles than configure servers. The two surfaces share a feature set; they differ in who operates the floor underneath.
