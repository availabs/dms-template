How NPMRDS data is sourced

The National Performance Management Research Data Set provides probe-derived travel times for every TMC segment on the National Highway System. NYSDOT ingests the feed nightly; the dashboard re-aggregates on the hour.

Quarterly publication

PM3 measures are computed annually but published quarterly so MPOs and academic partners can track in-year trends. See MAP-21 guidance for the measure definitions.

  • Level of Travel Time Reliability (LOTTR) — Interstate + NHS
  • Truck Travel Time Reliability (TTTR) — Interstate
  • Hours of Excessive Delay (HED) + Peak HED (PHED) — Urbanized Areas
