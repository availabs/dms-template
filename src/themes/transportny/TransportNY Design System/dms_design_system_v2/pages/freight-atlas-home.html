New York moves 936 million tons of freight a year. See where it goes.

The NYS Freight Atlas is the living, interactive companion to the 2024 New York State Freight Plan — every road, rail, port, airport, pipeline, and truck-parking layer the plan analyzes, on one map, refreshed to a 2021 base year and a 2050 forecast. Explore it, read the insights, and download the underlying data — free, no account required.

// 01 · what's new in the 2024 atlas

The plan refreshed the data. The Atlas shows it.

The 2024 plan tasks AVAIL with turning the Freight Web Atlas into a centralized, interactive, metadata-rich platform. Four headline changes drove this rebuild.

2021 / 2050 refresh

Baseline data, formerly "2015 or earlier," moves to a post-COVID 2021 TRANSEARCH base year with a 2050 forecast across every mode.

Truck parking

Appendix E's brand-new study — 216 in-state sites + 47 in a 15-mi buffer, hourly utilization, and ~55,000 undesignated ATRI stops — now a live layer.

FCN performance

TTTR, Total Excessive Delay, pavement IRI, bridge condition, and truck-crash overlays now ride on the Freight Core Network.

Equity & climate

USDOT Equitable Transportation Community (ETC) Index and CLCPA disadvantaged-community overlays become first-class map layers.
