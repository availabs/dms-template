Truck Parking Inventory.

production new 2024
version v2024 · current
// description

The Truck Parking Inventory catalogs every known commercial truck-parking facility in New York State, compiled for the 2024 NYS Freight Plan's Appendix E truck-parking study. It merges public rest areas and travel plazas with private truck stops, capturing capacity, amenities, and observed overnight utilization.

Each record carries ownership (public or private), designated truck spaces, an amenities list (bathroom, shower, food, EV charging), and a mean overnight occupancy rate derived from Trucker Path and satellite imagery. The 47 buffer sites fall within a 15-mile band outside the state line and are flagged for cross-border trip planning.

Use this layer to identify parking shortfalls along the Primary Freight Network, to site new capacity, and to support the federal Jason's Law parking-survey requirements.
