// methodology
How delay is measured and attributed

Excessive delay counts vehicle-hours spent below the congestion threshold (60% of posted speed, 20 mph floor), computed per TMC segment and month from NPMRDS 5-minute probe speeds. TRANSCOM events claim their share of the non-recurrent total — construction, accident, and other events — and recurrent delay is the remainder (total minus non-recurrent). Cost multiplies vehicle-hours by AADT-weighted occupancy and FHWA's value-of-time factor (≈ $18.8 per vehicle-hour in 2024). 2019–2020 attribution has not yet been computed and is shown as a gap, never interpolated; 2025 runs through October.
