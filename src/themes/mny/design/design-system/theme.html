Typography

Two-family system: Oswald (display — always uppercase) and Proxima Nova / Source Sans 3 (prose). 14 named tokens; modifier axes (color, weight, italic, tabular-nums) are applied at the call site, not as separate tokens.

Modifier axes — not separate tokens: color (text-[#37576B] / text-white / text-[#EAAD43]), weight (font-semibold / font-bold at call site), italic (prose + italic), tabular-nums (stat tables), uppercase on display/meta is always on — not a modifier. H1 heading underline: decoration-4 decoration-[#EAAD43] underline-offset-8 — applied at call site on displaySM.
