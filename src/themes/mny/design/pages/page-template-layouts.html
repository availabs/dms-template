About This Project

Project Alpha is a long-term infrastructure resilience initiative spanning three counties in the Northeast region. The project focuses on upgrading flood control systems along primary river corridors and improving drainage capacity in densely populated areas.

Funding is a combined federal-state partnership under the BRIC program, with a local cost-share of 25%. The current phase covers engineering design and environmental permitting, with construction expected to begin in Q2 2025.

For additional details, contact the Regional Program Coordinator or reference plan document BRIC-NY-2024-0041.
