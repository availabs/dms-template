Read the clusters, not the pins.

99.6% of these points are a town or county centroid, not a project site — so a single dot can stand for hundreds of actions. New York City alone stacks 2,022 actions on one point that never splits, however far you zoom. The donuts are the honest unit here; the individual pins are approximate. See the Actions Data Quality page for why.
